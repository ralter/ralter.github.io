About Me

Hi all, my name is Reuben. I currently study Mathematics at Colorado College. I figured it was worth tossing some information about me online -- nothing like internet permanence. My current interests are primarily in the field of computational mathematics - Numerical PDE's, Probability, and approximation/optimization algorithms. My hobby passion is everything cryptographic and digital security in our current era. I enjoy all of these fields immensely and will happily talk about them for hours if you let me.

Outside of math, I work at the National Center for Atmospheric Research on open-source weather sensing with the OpenIoTwx team. I also am employed at Colorado Colleges GIS center, mostly focusing on remote sensing and embedded systems programming (for drones).

The majority of the minimal time I have outside of academics, work, and personal projects is fanatically devoted to the outdoors - Ultrarunning, backcountry skiing, and climbing (when I'm not injured, like I've been recently).
